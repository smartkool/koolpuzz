KoolPuzz iOS App – Support & Instructions

Game Setup and Controls

  • Use the button or a zoom gesture to increase or decrease the game's difficulty level.
  • Tap the expand icon to make the picture fit the entire game scene.
  • Enable rotation of jigsaw pieces when the puzzle is created.
  • Shake your phone or tap the puzzle icon to mix up the jigsaw pieces and start playing.
  • Use a long-tap gesture to show the toolbar if it’s hidden.
  • Show/Hide the help screen by tapping the info icon.
  • Go back to the dashboard with the home icon.

Playing the Puzzle

  • Drag pieces over the game scene to reassemble the puzzle. If two pieces fit, they'll snap together automatically.
  • Double-tap a piece to rotate it.
  • Show the magnifier by long-tapping a piece before moving it.
  • Pinch to zoom for a closer look.
  • Use the grid icon to auto-calculate the best zoom level for an overview.
  • Show/Hide the original puzzle image using the eye icon.
  • Toggle sounds on/off with the music note icon.
  • Adjust background brightness with the sun icon.
