KoolPuzz iOS App – Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 16, 2025

Data Collection

  • We do not collect personal information directly within the app.
  • We do not use analytics or tracking tools to collect data ourselves.
  • We do not store or share personal user data.

Advertising and Consent

This app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. As a result, certain data may be collected automatically by Google and its partners, including device identifiers, usage data, and general location data, for ad delivery and analytics purposes.

We use Google’s User Messaging Platform (UMP) to display a consent message when you first launch the app, allowing you to choose whether to receive personalized ads or non-personalized ads. If you choose not to consent, only non-personalized ads will be shown.

You can learn more about how Google uses information from apps that use their services by visiting: Google Privacy & Terms.

Children’s Privacy

This app is safe for children and does not collect any personal information from users of any age. Ads displayed comply with Google’s policies for serving ads in apps for children.

Your Choices

You may manage your ad preferences at any time through your device settings or by contacting us. You may also request to reset your consent preferences within the app if you wish to change your choice regarding ad personalization.
